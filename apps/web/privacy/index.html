Legal

Privacy policy.

A plain-English summary of the personal data I collect when you enquire or visit, why I hold it, and the control you have over it.

Last updated: June 2026

Who I am

Beansprout is the trading name of a self-employed tattoo artist (sole trader). I run this website and I am the data controller for the personal information described in this policy. I work from Tiny Knives, a separate studio business at 41 Southgate Street, Winchester SO23 9EH; this policy covers only the information I hold as Beansprout, not anything Tiny Knives collects in its own right. I'm registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO); my registration reference is [ICO REGISTRATION REFERENCE]. As a tattoo artist I'm also registered with Winchester City Council under the Local Government (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act 1982 (registration number [TATTOO REGISTRATION NUMBER]). For any privacy question, email hello@beansprout.ink.

What I collect

When you enquire, book or visit, I collect the information you give me, which may include:

  • Contact details: your name, email address and (if you provide it) phone number.
  • Tattoo details: placement, size, your idea and any reference images you upload.
  • Health information you choose to share that's relevant to being tattooed safely.
  • Booking and consent records, including the consent form completed in studio.
  • Basic technical data needed to deliver the website and prevent abuse of my forms (see cookies and analytics below).

How I use it

I use your information to reply to your enquiry, agree and draw your design, arrange and deliver your appointment, take and manage deposits, follow up on aftercare, and keep the records I'm required to keep. If you sign up to the newsletter, I use your email only to send it, and only until you unsubscribe.

My lawful basis

I rely on different lawful bases under UK GDPR depending on the information: consent for the newsletter, and explicit consent for any health information you choose to share; performance of a contract (arranging and delivering your tattoo); legal obligation (records I must keep); and my legitimate interests in running Beansprout and keeping my website and forms secure. Where I rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

How long I keep it

I keep records only as long as I need them. Enquiries that don't lead to a booking are kept for up to 12 months and then deleted. Records relating to a tattoo I've carried out — including consent and any health details — are kept for the period required by my insurer and local-authority registration, normally up to a few years. Newsletter details are kept until you unsubscribe.

Who I share it with

I never sell your data. I share it only with the service providers that help me run Beansprout, and only as far as they need it: Cloudflare (which runs the enquiry and newsletter forms and securely stores submissions), Resend (which delivers my emails and holds the newsletter list), and GitHub Pages (which hosts this website). These act on my instructions under their own data-protection terms. Separately, the site loads its fonts from Google Fonts on every page, and the contact page embeds a Google map — when these load, your browser sends your IP address to Google, which handles it under its own privacy policy.

Cookies & analytics

The website doesn't use advertising or tracking cookies, and I don't run third-party analytics that profile you. My forms use a small amount of technical data (such as your IP address) purely to stop spam and abuse; this isn't used to identify or track you, and isn't shared for marketing. The only third-party embed is the Google map on the contact page, which Google may use to set its own cookies if you interact with it.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access the information I hold about you, to have it corrected or erased, to restrict or object to how I use it, to data portability, and to withdraw consent where I rely on it. To exercise any of these, email me using the details below and I'll respond within one month.

Changes & contact

I may update this policy from time to time; the date above shows the latest version. For any privacy question or request, email hello@beansprout.ink. If you're unhappy with how I've handled your data, you also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).
