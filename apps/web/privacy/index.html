Legal

Privacy policy.

A plain-English summary of what personal data we collect when you enquire or visit, why we hold it, and the control you have over it.

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Beansprout is a tattoo studio run by the artist trading as Beansprout, working from Tiny Knives, 41 Southgate Street, Winchester SO23 9EH. Beansprout is the data controller for the personal information described in this policy. For any privacy question, email hello@beansprout.ink.

What we collect

When you enquire, book or visit, we collect the information you give us, which may include:

  • Contact details: your name, email address and (if you provide it) phone number.
  • Tattoo details: placement, size, your idea and any reference images you upload.
  • Health information you choose to share that's relevant to being tattooed safely.
  • Booking and consent records, including the consent form completed in studio.
  • Basic technical data needed to deliver the website and prevent abuse of our forms (see cookies and analytics below).

How we use it

We use your information to reply to your enquiry, agree and draw your design, arrange and deliver your appointment, take and manage deposits, follow up on aftercare, and keep the records we're required to keep. If you sign up to the newsletter, we use your email only to send it, and only until you unsubscribe.

Our lawful basis

We rely on different lawful bases under UK GDPR depending on the information: consent (for example, the newsletter, and any health information you choose to share), performance of a contract (arranging and delivering your tattoo), legal obligation (records we must keep), and our legitimate interests in running the studio and keeping our website and forms secure. Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

How long we keep it

We keep records only as long as we need them. Enquiries that don't lead to a booking are kept for up to 12 months and then deleted. Records relating to a tattoo we've carried out — including consent and any health details — are kept for the period required by our insurer and local-authority registration, normally up to a few years. Newsletter details are kept until you unsubscribe.

Who we share it with

We never sell your data. We share it only with the service providers that help us run Beansprout, and only as far as they need it: Cloudflare (which runs the enquiry and newsletter forms and securely stores submissions), Resend (which delivers our emails and holds the newsletter list), and GitHub Pages (which hosts the website). These providers act on our instructions under their own data-protection terms.

Cookies & analytics

The website doesn't use advertising or tracking cookies. We don't run third-party analytics that profile you. Our forms use a small amount of technical data (such as your IP address) purely to stop spam and abuse; this isn't used to identify or track you, and isn't shared for marketing.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access the information we hold about you, to have it corrected or erased, to restrict or object to how we use it, to data portability, and to withdraw consent where we rely on it. To exercise any of these, email us using the details below and we'll respond within one month.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy from time to time; the date above shows the latest version. For any privacy question or request, email hello@beansprout.ink. You also have the right to complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).
