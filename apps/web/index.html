How it works

From idea to healed.

From your first message to your healed tattoo, here's how a piece comes together.

01

Enquire

Send me your idea, rough size, placement and any references. The more you tell me, the better I can picture it.

02

Design

Once we've agreed the brief and a date, I draw your piece. You'll see it the night before your appointment.

03

Your session

We settle in and take it at your pace, breaks and all. The time is yours, so there's no rushing.

04

Aftercare

You leave wrapped, with clear instructions for looking after it, and I'll check in to see how it's healing.
