Legal

Terms of service.

The agreement between you and Beansprout when you book: deposits, cancellations, suitability, and what's expected on both sides.

Last updated: June 2026

Booking & enquiries

Submitting an enquiry doesn't reserve a date. Your appointment is confirmed only once I've agreed the design and a date with you and your deposit has been paid. Until then, dates remain open to other clients.

Deposits

A deposit is required to book and is deducted from the final cost of your tattoo. It pays for the time spent designing your piece and securing your slot, so it's non-refundable if you cancel or don't attend. The balance is payable on the day.

Cancellations & rescheduling

Please give as much notice as you can. With at least 72 hours' notice I'll move your appointment and your deposit carries over to the new date. With less notice, or for a missed appointment, the deposit is forfeited and a new one is needed to rebook.

Age & ID

You must be 18 or over and bring valid photo ID to your appointment. I cannot tattoo anyone underage under any circumstances, and parental consent makes no difference to this. If you can't show ID, I won't be able to proceed and standard cancellation terms apply.

Health & suitability

Please tell me in advance about any relevant health conditions, medication, allergies or pregnancy, so I can keep you safe. I may decline, adapt or postpone work where it isn't safe to proceed, or ask for a doctor's note first. You must not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs at your appointment.

Aftercare & touch-ups

How well a tattoo heals depends on following the aftercare guidance, which is your responsibility once you leave. One free touch-up is offered within six months where the aftercare has been followed. Touch-ups don't cover fading from sun exposure or picking, or changes of mind about the design.

Designs & copyright

Designs are drawn custom for one client and one tattoo. The original artwork remains my intellectual property and isn't sold, reused or licensed for reproduction elsewhere. I may decline to repeat a design for someone else.

Photography & social media

I may photograph finished work for the portfolio and social channels, but only with your permission, which you give (or decline) on the enquiry form and can withdraw at any time by emailing me. Withdrawing permission doesn't affect anything already printed, and I never share your personal details alongside an image.

Liability

I carry out every tattoo to professional hygiene and safety standards. Some normal effects of tattooing — short-term swelling, minor variation in healing, and natural fading over time — aren't faults. Nothing in these terms limits my liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, or anything else it would be unlawful to exclude.

Changes & contact

Beansprout is the trading name of a self-employed tattoo artist working from Tiny Knives, 41 Southgate Street, Winchester SO23 9EH. I may update these terms from time to time; the date above shows the latest version. Questions? Email hello@beansprout.ink or see the FAQ.
