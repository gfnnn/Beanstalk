Before you visit

What to expect on the day

  • The studio is up a flight of stairs and isn't step-free. If that's a barrier for you, message me before you book and we'll find a way to make it work.
  • Come at your appointment time and buzz the door. I'll come down to let you in.
  • Bring photo ID. I can only tattoo over-18s, so I'll need to check it before we start.
  • Your deposit comes off the final price; the balance is due when we finish.
  • Eat beforehand and stay hydrated, and wear comfy clothes that give easy access to the area we're working on.
  • We'll take breaks whenever you need them. Just say the word.
